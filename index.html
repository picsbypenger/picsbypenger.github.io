about me

Hi! My name is Derek Peng and I'm a multidisciplinary photographer and designer in the Raleigh area. I primarily shoot digital, but I've fallen in love with film as well. I seek out unique narratives and reveal them through my photography; for me, some ideas and emotions are better conveyed through pictures than words. I lose myself exploring techniques and tackling ambitious projects, absorbed in a pursuit of perfection that's uniquely mine. Ultimately, I want to inspire people to challenge and change our world.

Follow my work and contact me for inquiries on Instagram. Visit the directory for a complete list of posts.
