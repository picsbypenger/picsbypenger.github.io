about me

Hi! My name is Derek Peng and I'm a multidisciplinary photographer and designer in the Ames area. I primarily shoot digital, but I've fallen in love with film as well. I find that everything has a story to tell. I seek out these unique narratives and reveal them through my photography; for me, some ideas and emotions are better conveyed through pictures than words. Ultimately, I want to challenge people to think differentlyâ€”my photographs themselves may not change the world, but they can inspire the individuals who can.

Photography is my escape. I lose myself in new techniques and ambitious projects, absorbed in a pursuit of perfection that's uniquely mine. In photography, I take refuge from the world of expectations. Each work is a reflection of myself, always evolving. My photographs document fun adventures and close-calls, remind me of challenges I overcame and worries long gone. They are as much a chronicle of my life as they are a chronicle of the world around me.
