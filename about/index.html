about

Derek Peng is an Iowa raised, North Carolina based photographer. He began shooting as a teenager, documenting stories for his high school's yearbook. Over the years, Derek has crafted his own style of photography, combining the raw emotion of each moment with a dreamlike touch. He is committed to dreaming big and remaining curious—always learning, never settling.
